§ 06 — THE FIRM
About Seckin

Senior-led international trade
and advisory execution
from Istanbul.

Seckin was built around a simple conviction: that the best trading firms and the best advisors behave the same way — they are patient with people and impatient with paperwork.
We run a senior-only desk. Every mandate is carried by someone who has sat in front of a bank, a customs officer and a counterparty — often in the same week. That's the only way we know how to work.

Seckin International Trade & Consulting is an Istanbul-registered firm working across five interlocking disciplines: commodity trade, business consulting, project management, financial services and business development.

We keep the partnership small on purpose. A lean team, a shared file, and the discipline to say no to mandates outside our circle of competence. The upside: you always speak to a decision-maker, documentation turns within the business day, and nothing gets lost in translation.

Our clients include mid-sized producers, sovereign-linked buyers, family offices, and international principals looking for a discreet presence in Turkey, the Gulf and beyond.
